Zesty: Crafting the Future of Social Cooking

Background

As the COVID-19 pandemic unfolded, countless individuals rediscovered their passion for home cooking. To meet their need for a centralized platform to organize, share, and explore recipes, Zesty was conceived - a social cooking platform that empowers home cooks, ignites inspiration, and streamlines recipe management.

Objective: Building a User-Centric Platform

Leveraging my expertise in UX/UI design, web development, and data analysis, I aimed to create an intuitive social cooking platform that simplifies recipe creation, organization, and sharing. Try it yourself!

Data-Driven Market Validation

To assess the demand for a platform like Zesty, I utilized my research skills to survey a representative sample of 900 active home cooks using CloudResearch. The study revealed that 83% of respondents use multiple methods to organize their recipes, with the majority expressing dissatisfaction with their current system. An impressive 87% expressed interest in using Zesty to manage their recipes.

User-Focused Recipe Creation Flow

Employing an iterative design process, I conducted 30+ usability tests over two months to perfect the recipe creation workflow. This user-centric approach allowed me to refine the platform's design and maximize user experience, demonstrating my commitment to delivering a product that meets users' needs and expectations.

Creating recipes in Zesty consists of five steps:

  1. Enter the recipe name and choose between public or private visibility
  2. Select the cuisine type, cooking time, recipe yield, and relevant tags
  3. Add ingredients and their corresponding quantities
  4. Provide step-by-step preparation instructions
  5. Optionally, upload a photo of the finished dish

Innovative Recipe Importing Feature

Capitalizing on my Python programming skills, I developed a script to scrape metadata from various recipe websites, allowing users to effortlessly import recipes from their favorite sources into the Zesty platform. Currently, I am spearheading the development of a new feature that enables recipe imports based on images. The front-end design is under construction, with plans to launch this innovative feature in the coming year.
