About Me

I'm a data-driven developer with a strong background in social psychological research. I specialize in designing research methods, processing and analyzing data, and creating impactful visualizations to drive insightful decisions. With expertise in R, Python, SQL, and various data visualization tools, I'm committed to delivering cutting-edge solutions for your business needs.

Research & Data Analysis

With a background in social psychological research, I design tailored research methods to generate relevant data. I use my expertise in R, Python, and SQL to employ state-of-the-art quantitative methods, ranging from linear and nonlinear regressions, multilevel modeling, natural language processing, machine learning, to deep neural networks. This allows me to generate important research insights and make data-driven decisions.

Data Visualization & Communication

I believe that effective communication is key to driving impactful insights. I use various data visualization tools, such as ggplot2, Matplotlib, and Tableau, to create visually appealing and informative graphics. My excellent communication and presentation skills enable me to clearly and effectively explain my findings to a variety of audiences, including colleagues, clients, and stakeholders.
